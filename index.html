Rockfall!

Controls:

  • Left/Right arrows: go left/right
  • s: Super science slow mo'
  • p: Pause
  • n: New game

Info:

Survive until the timer runs out to win. The game will get harder, starting at level 1, although if you die you'll drop to level 0. You begin with two hearts and lose one if struck by a rock. If you are struck with no hearts, you will die.
